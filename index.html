Dr. Tina Gingrich, OBGYN


At Wildflower Women's Center, we understand that you are the most important part of our practice. We work in partnership with you, providing everything you need from routine preventive care to the most serious medical intervention. We pride ourselves in using the most up-to-date information, diagnostic tools, and interventions to provide the least invasive care allowing you the quickest, most comfortable recovery.

Dr. Gingrich and her staff believe that your pregnancy and delivery is the most precious time in your life. We strive to help you realize the birthing experience for which you dream. Using the latest medical information, the medical staff will partner with you to keep you and your baby healthy-- and be with you when your baby arrives.
